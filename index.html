Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island National Seashore is a peaceful escape from the city.

Highlights include walking through the unique Sunken Forest, climbing the historic Fire Island Lighthouse, and relaxing on beautiful beaches.

It is a wonderful experience full of nature, history, and tranquility.
